About Text Statistics

Text Statistics analyzes text to provide comprehensive metrics including character count, word count, sentence count, paragraph count, reading time, and more. These statistics are essential for writers meeting word limits, content creators optimizing for platforms, and anyone needing to understand text composition. The tool updates statistics in real-time as you type or paste content.

The analyzer counts various text elements: total characters (with and without spaces), words, sentences, paragraphs, lines, and unique words. It calculates estimated reading time based on average reading speed, average word length, and longest word. The tool handles multiple languages and provides accurate counts even with complex punctuation and formatting.

Common Use Cases

Writers track word counts for articles, essays, and manuscripts with specific length requirements. Students verify assignment word limits. Social media managers optimize posts for platform character limits. SEO specialists analyze content length for search optimization. Translators estimate project scope. Editors assess text complexity. Content creators plan reading time for blog posts and scripts.

Paste or type your text into the input field. Statistics update automatically in real-time, showing all metrics instantly. All analysis happens locally in your browser.
